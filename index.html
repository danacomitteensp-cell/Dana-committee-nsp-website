Transforming Committee Management

The Dana Committee App represents a significant step forward in modernizing and streamlining community contribution management. Our objective is to provide a secure, reliable, and structured digital environment for managing member data, credentials, contributions, and event activities with clarity and confidence.

Key Capabilities

Credential Access

Each member receives secure, individualized access to their login credentials, ensuring privacy and eliminating dependency on manual sharing.

Contribution Tracking

Maintain accurate and transparent records of member contributions, allowing committees to monitor data effortlessly and reduce administrative workload.

Miqaat & Member Management

Create and manage events, track attendance, and access member information with ease , all optimized for mobile use.
